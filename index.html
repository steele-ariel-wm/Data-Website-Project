People with Disabilities

A disability is a physical or mental condition that limits a person's movements, senses, and/or activities. Some of the most common are autism, ADD, down syndrome, cerebral palsy and a couple others.

The employment rate of people with disabilities is very low.

The disability rate is much higher than children with disabilities. Disabilities aren't just down syndrome or autism. Disabilities are anything that limits someone's movements, senses, and/or activities.

Three specific categories of disabilities are vision difficulties, hearing difficulties, and difficulty concentrating, remembering, or making decisions. Most people with a disability have difficulty concentrating, remembering, or making decisions.
